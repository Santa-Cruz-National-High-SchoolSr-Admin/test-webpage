Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track

Gain practical skills and TESDA certifications for immediate employment opportunities

Computer Systems Servicing (CSS)

Learn computer hardware and software maintenance, troubleshooting, and basic networking. Get hands-on experience with computer systems and earn TESDA certification.

Core Competencies

  • Computer Hardware Installation
  • Operating System Configuration
  • Computer Systems Maintenance
  • Basic Networking

TESDA Certification

  • NC II - Computer Systems Servicing

Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM)

Master electrical wiring installation and maintenance for residential and commercial buildings. Learn safety procedures and electrical system troubleshooting.

Core Competencies

  • Electrical Wiring Installation
  • Electrical Systems Testing
  • Safety Procedures
  • Blueprint Reading

TESDA Certification

  • NC II - Electrical Installation and Maintenance

Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW)

Learn professional welding techniques and metal fabrication. Develop skills in various welding positions and safety protocols.

Core Competencies

  • Basic Welding
  • Metal Fabrication
  • Welding Safety
  • Quality Control

TESDA Certification

  • NC II - Shielded Metal Arc Welding

Food and Beverage Services (FBS)

Learn professional food service operations, customer service, and food safety standards. Prepare for a career in the hospitality industry.

Core Competencies

  • Food Service Operations
  • Customer Service
  • Food Safety & Sanitation
  • Beverage Preparation

TESDA Certification

  • NC II - Food and Beverage Services

Bread and Pastry Production (BPP)

Master the art of baking and pastry making. Learn professional techniques, recipe development, and food safety standards.

Core Competencies

  • Basic Baking
  • Pastry Making
  • Food Safety
  • Recipe Development

TESDA Certification

  • NC II - Bread and Pastry Production
